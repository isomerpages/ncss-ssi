<h3><strong>Foundation to Advanced Crisis Response Training</strong></h3>
<p>Delivered in collaboration with the <strong>Crisis Prevention Institute (CPI®)</strong>,
the CPI Train-the-Trainer Certification includes programmes that equip
you to deliver internationally recognised, evidence-based crisis response
training using trauma-informed approaches that prioritise <em>Care, Welfare, Safety, and Security<sup>SM</sup></em>.</p>
<p>Building comprehensive crisis response training capacity, the CPI Train-the-Trainer
Certification includes:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>CPI <em>Safety Intervention™</em> Foundation Training Instructor Certification
Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CPI <em>Safety Intervention™</em> Advanced and Emergency Training Instructor
Certification Programme <em>(Coming Soon!)</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CPI <em>Verbal Intervention™</em> Training Instructor Certification Programme <em>(Coming Soon!)</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CPI <em>Safety Intervention™</em> Foundation Training Instructor Certification
Programme - Renewal</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4><strong>CPI <em>Safety Intervention™</em> Foundation Training Instructor Certification Programme</strong></h4>
<p>This comprehensive instructor certification covers the complete spectrum
of crisis response skills, equipping you to deliver training in both verbal
de-escalation and physical intervention techniques. As the foundation of
CPI training, this programme provides the essential credentials and expertise
needed to train others in evidence-based crisis response. It is recommended
for professionals who work in environments that require restrictive or
physical intervention techniques.</p>
<p><strong>What You'll Learn:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprehensive crisis management including verbal de-escalation strategies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Physical intervention techniques for crisis situations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Trauma-informed approaches to crisis response</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Risk assessment and decision-making frameworks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Professional and legal considerations in crisis intervention</p>
</li>
</ul>
