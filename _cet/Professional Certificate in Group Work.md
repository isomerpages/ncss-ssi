<h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3>
<p>Jointly developed with the Ministry of Social and Family Development (MSF)
and Ministry of Health (MOH), 'Professional Certificate in Group Work'
consists of two integrated modules that will provide social workers with
foundation-level practice competencies. Learners will be equipped with
the competencies to assess the need for group work in a social work setting
and plan appropriate interventions in a group setting.</p>
<p>Social workers can embark on this course as part of a competency-based
training roadmap to deepen their skills and knowledge and plan their career
progression pathway based on competencies and job roles.</p>
<h3><strong>Target Audience</strong></h3>
<p>Social Workers, Youth Workers and other Social Work Professionals who
are either:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>New to group work, and looking to equip themselves with the relevant skills
and knowledge to deliver group work effectively.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experienced in using group work interventions, but desire to broaden or
deepen their skillsets to deliver group work more effectively.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Assessment Requirements</strong></h3>
<p>Learners are required to undergo summative assessments in the form of
role play and written assessments. All learners are required to attend
an oral interview at the end of each module.</p>
<h3><strong>Course Details</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Module 1: Group Work Assessment and Planning</strong>
<br>This module will provide learners with the competencies to assess the
need for group work and plan appropriate interventions for a group. The
key purpose of this module is to equip learners with the relevant knowledge
and practice skills to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Discern if there is a need for group work.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plan a support group to address the needs of the group.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Design effective group work programmes and activities (interventions)
to address the needs of a specific group.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Module 2: Group Work Intervention</strong>
<br>This module will provide learners with the competencies to administer
group work. The key purpose of this module is to equip learners with the
relevant knowledge and practice skills to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Form an appropriate group for the group work.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Obtain resources to deliver the group work.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conduct group interventions effectively.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage issues, conflicts and risks that may arise during group sessions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitor the progress and effectiveness of the group and group work.</p>
</li>
</ul>
