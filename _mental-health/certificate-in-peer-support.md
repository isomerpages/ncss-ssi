<h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3>
<p>This is the second module of the "Peer Support Specialist Programme".
It equips learners with the necessary competencies to perform the role
of a Peer Support Specialist.</p>
<p>This module comprises of 10 days of classroom training and 100 hours of
supervised practicum in a social service or healthcare agency. Learners
who fulfil all the requirements for this module and are assessed to be
competent will receive a Certificate of Achievement in Peer Support.</p>
<p>It is preferred that the learner has completed the module, "Preparatory
Training for Potential Peer Support Specialist", by SSI which is the first
module in the Peer Support Specialist Programme.</p>
<h3><strong>Target Audience</strong></h3>
<p>The target audience for this module should be:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Keen to build on their competencies in order to perform the role of a
Peer Support Specialist.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At least 21 years old.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have a diagnosed mental health condition that has stabilised.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Willing to share their own lived experiences to support others in their
recovery.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Self-identifying as a present or former primary consumer of mental health
services.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Learning Outcomes</strong></h3>
<p>Upon completion of this module, learners are expected to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Apply recovery and resilience principles to support their peers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate peer support practice in the role of a peer support specialist.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify key areas of progress of their peers and be able to notate the
progress of peer support sessions through relevant documentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply the practice of peer support and resilient conversation in culturally
diverse situations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply ethical standards to peer support scenarios.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Relate a personal recovery story from a resilience perspective.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Facilitate a healthcare presentation for peer support.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Facilitate peer support through a resilient-focused conversations in diverse
situations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Assessment Requirements</strong></h3>
<p>Learners will be assessed through a mid-term written assessment, a practical
assessment and a final written assessment. Learners are also required to
undergo 100 hours of practicum and assessment, upon completion of the classroom
training.</p>
